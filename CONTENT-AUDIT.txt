[PAGE HEADER]
- Title: "Frequently Asked Questions"

--- THE APP ---

Q: What is Pixie Journey?
A: Pixie Journey is a health tracking app that uses cute stickers instead of boring dropdowns. Track your mood, sleep, energy, meals, and 15+ other categories with just a tap. See your whole year at a glance with our year-in-pixels calendar.

Q: When does it launch?
A: Q1 2026 (January-March 2026) for iOS in Australia. We'll expand to other countries and Android after the initial launch.

Q: Can I go back and log past days?
A: Absolutely! There's no time limit. Go back weeks, months, or years if you want. We don't do guilt-based "streaks" that punish you for missing days.

Q: Do I have to add notes every day?
A: Nope. Notes are completely optional. Tap a sticker, done. Some days you'll want to add context. Other days you won't have the energy. Both are completely okay.

Q: What if I miss a week (or a month)?
A: Then you miss a week. No shame, no broken streaks, no annoying notifications. Come back when you're ready. Lalah will be here.

Q: Will there be an Android version?
A: Yes, eventually. We're starting with iOS because it's faster to build and launch. Android will come after we stabilize the iOS version.

--- PRICING & TIERS ---

Q: Is Free tier really free?
A: Yes! Free tier is completely free forever. No time limit, no credit card required, no hidden fees. Track 4 categories for as long as you want.

Q: What's the difference between Free and Life tier?
A: Free tier (4 categories): Mood, Sleep, Hydration, Fertility
   Life tier (19 categories total): Everything in Free + Energy, Exercise, Meals, Screen Time, Self-Care, Social, Gratitude, Weather, Spending, Chores, Creativity, Appointments, Work Tasks, Errands, Events. Plus no ads and data export.

Q: How does waitlist pricing work?
A: All waitlist members get the Lifetime tier for $14.99 instead of the regular $19.99 (save $5). This pricing is locked in for you when we launch—no time limit, no rush, no countdown.

Q: Lifetime vs subscription — which should I choose?
A: Subscription ($4.99/month): Lower upfront cost, cancel anytime, good if you want to try it first.
   Lifetime ($14.99 for waitlist): One-time payment, better value after 3 months, never worry about subscription renewals.

Q: What happens to my data if I stop paying?
A: If you cancel your Life tier subscription, you automatically move to the Free tier. You keep all your historical data, but can only actively log the 4 Free tier categories going forward. You can export all your data anytime before canceling.

--- FEATURES & TRACKING ---

Q: What can I track with Pixie Journey?
A: Free tier: Mood, Sleep, Hydration, Fertility
   Life tier: All Free categories PLUS Energy, Exercise, Meals, Screen Time, Self-Care, Social, Gratitude, Weather, Spending, Chores, Creativity, Appointments, Work Tasks, Errands, Events
   Clinical tier (coming later): 31+ medical conditions including pain management, medications, migraine, anxiety, depression, and more.

Q: What's "year-in-pixels"?
A: A year-in-pixels calendar shows all 365 days of your year in a grid, with each day represented by a tiny colored pixel based on what you logged. At a glance, you can see patterns emerge—clusters of good days, tough weeks, seasonal changes. It's like seeing your whole year as a piece of art.

Q: Can I export my data?
A: Yes, with Life tier and above. You can export to CSV format to use in spreadsheets, share with doctors, or keep as backup.

Q: Is there cloud sync?
A: Optional. Your data stores locally on your device by default (privacy-first). You can optionally enable cloud sync to backup data or access from multiple devices.

--- PRIVACY & SAFETY ---

Q: Is my data private?
A: Yes. Your health data stays yours. We don't sell it, we don't share it with advertisers, and we don't use it to train AI models. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Q: Where is my data stored?
A: Your data is stored locally on your device by default. If you enable optional cloud sync, encrypted data is stored on Australian servers.

Q: Can I delete my data?
A: Yes, anytime. Go to Settings → Delete All Data. This is permanent and cannot be undone.

--- FERTILITY & PERIOD TRACKING ---

Q: Can I use this for birth control?
A: NO. Pixie Journey is for cycle awareness only. It is NOT contraception and should NOT be used to prevent pregnancy. Use proven contraceptive methods if you don't want to get pregnant.

Q: Why is fertility tracking free?
A: We believe reproductive health information shouldn't be paywalled. Period tracking and basic fertility awareness data are free for everyone, forever.

--- CLINICAL TIER ---

Q: What's Clinical tier?
A: Clinical tier adds 31+ medical condition categories with multi-symptom quick logging, automatic timestamps, medical reports for doctors, and pattern detection. It's for people managing chronic conditions who need medical-grade tracking.

Q: When does Clinical tier launch?
A: Q2 2026 (April-June 2026). Pricing will be announced closer to launch. Join the waitlist and indicate "Clinical tier interest" to be notified.

--- TECHNICAL ---

Q: What iOS version do I need?
A: iOS 15 or later. We recommend iOS 16+ for best performance.

Q: Does it work offline?
A: Yes! The app works completely offline. Cloud sync requires internet, but all core tracking works without connection.

Q: Why Australia only at first?
A: We're an Australian company and need to ensure we comply with local privacy laws before expanding globally. Australia-first also lets us launch faster and iterate based on feedback.
