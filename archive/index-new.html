Built By People Who Get It

We built Pixie Journey because we desperately needed it.

We understand what tracking tools get wrong: they're built for people whose brains cooperate, whose days fit neat boxes, who can handle guilt when they miss a day.

We built the tracker that works for real humans on their worst days.
