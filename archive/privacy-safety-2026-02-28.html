Privacy & Safety

Your safety and privacy matter to us.

In Crisis? Get Help Now

Pixie Journey is NOT a crisis service.
If you're in immediate danger, please contact emergency services or a crisis helpline.

Australia:

  • Emergency: 000
  • Lifeline (24/7): 13 11 14 | lifeline.org.au
  • Beyond Blue (24/7): 1300 22 4636 | beyondblue.org.au
  • Suicide Call Back Service (24/7): 1300 659 467
  • Kids Helpline (ages 5-25): 1800 55 1800
  • 1800RESPECT (DV/sexual violence): 1800 737 732

United States:

  • Emergency: 911
  • 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline: Call/text 988
  • Crisis Text Line: Text "HELLO" to 741741

United Kingdom:

  • Emergency: 999
  • Samaritans (24/7): 116 123
  • Shout: Text "SHOUT" to 85258

New Zealand:

  • Emergency: 111
  • Lifeline (24/7): 0800 543 354
  • 1737: Free call/text 1737

Your Privacy Matters

We built Pixie Journey with privacy as a core principle:

What We DON'T Do:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Share your health data with advertisers
  • Use your data to train AI models
  • Share data with insurance companies
  • Track you across other apps or websites

What We DO:

  • Store your data locally on your device by default
  • Encrypt data in transit and at rest
  • Give you full control over your data
  • Let you export or delete your data anytime
  • Comply with Australian Privacy Act

Read our full Privacy Policy for details.

How We Protect Your Data

Local Storage (Default):

By default, your tracking data lives only on your device. We can't see it, we can't access it. It's yours.

Optional Cloud Sync:

If you enable cloud sync:

  • Data is encrypted in transit (TLS/SSL)
  • Data is stored in Australia
  • We use industry-standard security practices
  • You can disable sync and delete cloud data anytime

Safety for Vulnerable Users

If You're in an Unsafe Situation:

  • App Lock: Use your device's built-in app lock or biometric security
  • Private Notes: Don't include identifying details if someone else might access your device
  • Cloud Sync: Consider disabling if you're concerned about account access
  • Export Regularly: Keep backups in a safe location only you control
  • Delete Anytime: You can delete all data instantly in Settings
