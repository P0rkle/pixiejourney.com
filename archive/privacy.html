Privacy & Safety

Last updated: January 31, 2026

Quick Summary

  • On-device processing: Information you type into our tools is processed in your browser on your device. We don't receive or store those entries.
  • No account required: You can use the website tools without creating an account.
  • Minimal data collection: We collect limited technical data needed to operate, protect, and improve the website.
  • Not medical advice: Our content and tools provide general information only.
  • Not for emergencies: If you need urgent help, contact local emergency services.

1. Privacy-First Design

On-device processing

When you use a Pixie tool on this website, the calculations and formatting happen locally in your browser. That means:

  • We do not receive your tool inputs (e.g., dates, symptoms, scores, notes)
  • We do not store those tool inputs on our servers
  • Your entries stay on your device unless you choose to share them

No account required

You can use the website tools without logging in or creating an account.

Downloads and exports

If you download or export a PDF or image from the website:

  • The file is saved on your device
  • Be careful when sharing exports, as they may contain sensitive personal information

2. What We Collect (and Why)

Even privacy-first websites receive some technical information when you visit. We may collect limited data such as:

  • Device and browser type
  • Approximate location (e.g., country/region) inferred from IP address
  • Pages visited and basic usage patterns (to keep the site working and understand what's useful)
  • Security and performance logs (to detect abuse, prevent attacks, and troubleshoot issues)
  • Error logs (to fix bugs)

Purpose: We use this information to operate the site, improve reliability, and keep the website secure.

3. What We Do Not Collect

We do not collect or store:

  • The specific entries you type into our on-site tools (including health-related entries), unless you explicitly send them to us (for example, by emailing us)
  • Passwords for the website tools (because no login is required)
  • Advertising profiles or cross-site tracking identifiers

4. Cookies & Tracking

We aim to keep tracking minimal and privacy-respecting.

  • Essential cookies only: We use essential cookies required for core website functionality and security.
  • Privacy-focused analytics: We use minimal, privacy-focused analytics to understand general site usage in aggregate.
  • No advertising pixels: We do not use advertising pixels and we do not sell personal data.

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

5. Third-Party Services and Links

Service providers

We may use reputable third-party providers (for example: website hosting, content delivery, and basic security services). These providers may process limited technical data to deliver the website.

External links

Our website may link to other websites (for example: clinics, resources, app stores, or partners). We are not responsible for the content, availability, security, or privacy practices of third-party sites. Please review their policies before sharing information.

6. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the website, including:

  • Using HTTPS to encrypt traffic in transit
  • Monitoring for abuse and attacks
  • Applying updates and security patches as needed

Important limits

No website can be guaranteed 100% secure. Using the site is at your own risk, and you should avoid sharing sensitive information on any device or network you do not trust.

Report a security issue

If you believe you've found a security vulnerability, please contact us:

Security contact: security@pixiejourney.com

7. Health & Information Disclaimer

Pixie Journey provides general information and self-tracking tools for personal use. It is not a medical device, does not provide medical advice, and is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease.

Always seek advice from a qualified health professional for questions about your health. Never delay or ignore professional advice because of something you read or generate on this website.

8. Not for Emergencies

Pixie Journey is not an emergency service. If you believe you are experiencing a medical emergency, contact your local emergency number immediately.

Australia emergency contacts

  • Emergency: 000
  • Lifeline: 13 11 14
  • Beyond Blue: 1300 22 4636
  • Suicide Call Back Service: 1300 659 467

International: Find crisis resources at findahelpline.com

9. Accuracy and Reliability

We work to keep the site reliable, but we do not guarantee that:

  • Tools, templates, or outputs are error-free
  • Information is complete, current, or suitable for every person or situation
  • Results will match real-world outcomes

Use your judgment and verify important information with a professional.
