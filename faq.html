Frequently Asked Questions

Free Tracking PDFs

What do I get?

Four beautifully illustrated Lalah tracker coloring pages as print-ready PDFs: Mood Star Tracker, Sleep Potion Tracker, Macaron Tracker (universal), and Garden Patch Tracker (universal). Each PDF includes a bonus dot grid page on the back for notes or extra tracking.

Are the tracking PDFs free?

Yes — completely free. Enter your email and we'll send you a download link.

What size are the PDFs?

True A4 (210 x 297 mm) at 300 DPI — designed for Australian standard paper. They'll also print on US Letter with very slight cropping at the top and bottom.

How do I print them correctly?

For best results:

  • Paper size: A4
  • Scale: Set to "Actual size" or "100%" (not "Fit to page" — that shrinks the artwork and adds extra margins)
  • Colour: These are coloring pages, so black and white is perfect
How do I print double-sided so page 2 isn't upside down?

Each PDF has 2 pages — the tracker on the front and a dot grid on the back. To print them double-sided correctly:

  • If your printer has automatic duplex: Choose "Print on both sides" and select "Flip on long edge" (this is the correct setting for portrait pages)
  • If you're flipping paper manually: Print page 1 first. Then put the paper back in the tray with the printed side face-up and the top of the page going in first. Print page 2. (This can vary by printer — do a test page first!)
  • If in doubt: Just print single-sided. Two separate pages work great too.
Can I use them digitally (GoodNotes / Notability)?

Yes — import the PDF into your note app and colour digitally with Apple Pencil or stylus.

What do I track with them?

The mood and sleep trackers have built-in legends. The macaron and garden patch trackers are universal — use them for anything: habits, water intake, medication, exercise, or a simple "good day / hard day" colour key.

I didn't receive the email — what should I do?

Check your Promotions or Spam folder, then try again. If it still doesn't arrive, contact us and we'll send them directly.

The App

What is Pixie Journey?

Pixie Journey is a cosy Life in Pixels tracking system for bullet journal lovers. Fill one square a day (or backfill anytime) and watch patterns appear over time — with no streaks and no pressure.

When does it launch?

Q3 2026 (July-September 2026) for iOS in Australia. We'll expand to other countries and Android after the initial launch.

Can I go back and log past days?

Absolutely! There's no time limit. Go back weeks, months, or years if you want. We don't do guilt-based "streaks" that punish you for missing days.

Do I have to add notes every day?

Nope. Notes are completely optional. Tap a sticker, done. Some days you'll want to add context. Other days you won't have the energy. Both are completely okay.

What if I miss a week (or a month)?

Then you miss a week. No shame, no broken streaks, no annoying notifications. Come back when you're ready. Lalah will be here.

Will there be an Android version?

Yes, eventually. We're starting with iOS because it's faster to build and launch. Android will come after we stabilize the iOS version.

Will Pixie Journey have streaks or shame reminders?

No. The system is designed to be gentle and flexible. No streaks, no guilt, no punishing notifications.

Pricing & Tiers

Is Life Free really free?

Yes! Life Free is completely free forever. No time limit, no credit card required, no hidden fees. Track up to 5 categories + 1 planning for as long as you want.

What's the difference between Life Free and Life+?

Life Free: Core tracking categories — enough to get started.

Life+: All 20+ categories, no ads, and data export. Full details coming closer to launch.

How does waitlist pricing work?

All waitlist members get Life+ Lifetime for $14.99 instead of the regular $49.99 (save $35). This pricing is locked in for you when we launch—no time limit, no rush, no countdown.

Lifetime vs subscription — which should I choose?

Subscription ($4.99/month): Lower upfront cost, cancel anytime, good if you want to try it first.

Lifetime ($14.99 for waitlist): One-time payment, better value after 3 months, never worry about subscription renewals.

What happens to my data if I stop paying?

If you cancel your Life+ subscription, you automatically move to Life Free. You keep all your historical data, but can only actively log the Life Free categories going forward. You can export all your data anytime before canceling.

Features & Tracking

What will the app track?

Pixie Journey starts you with 6 free categories:

  • Mood (5 levels)
  • Sleep (quality + hours)
  • Binary Wellness (yes/no daily check-ins like hydration, exercise, medication)
  • Planning (daily intentions)
  • Custom (create your own)
  • Period tracking (basic)

Upgrade to Life+ for 20+ categories including energy, pain, anxiety, social, appetite, focus, intimacy, fertility, symptoms, substances, gratitude, and more.

Every entry supports optional context notes — capture the why behind the data, not just the what.

Can I export my data?

Yes, with Life+ and above. You can export to CSV format to use in spreadsheets, share with doctors, or keep as backup.

Is there cloud sync?

Optional. Your data stores locally on your device by default (privacy-first). You can optionally enable cloud sync to backup data or access from multiple devices.

Privacy & Safety

Is my data private?

Yes. Your health data stays yours. We don't sell it, we don't share it with advertisers, and we don't use it to train AI models. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Where is my data stored?

Your data is stored locally on your device by default. If you enable optional cloud sync, encrypted data is stored on Australian servers.

Can I delete my data?

Yes, anytime. Go to Settings → Delete All Data. This is permanent and cannot be undone.

Fertility & Period Tracking

Can I use this for birth control?

NO. Pixie Journey is for cycle awareness only. It is NOT contraception and should NOT be used to prevent pregnancy. Use proven contraceptive methods if you don't want to get pregnant.

Why is fertility tracking free?

We believe reproductive health information shouldn't be paywalled. Period tracking and basic fertility awareness data are free for everyone, forever.

Clinical Tier (Coming 2027)

What's Clinical tier?

A future tier for people managing chronic conditions — with multi-entry timestamps, medical reports, and more. Details coming closer to launch.

When does Clinical tier launch?

2027. Pricing will be announced closer to launch. Join the waitlist and indicate "Clinical tier interest" to be notified.

Technical

What iOS version do I need?

iOS 15 or later. We recommend iOS 16+ for best performance.

Does it work offline?

Yes! The app works completely offline. Cloud sync requires internet, but all core tracking works without connection.

Why Australia only at first?

We're an Australian company and need to ensure we comply with local privacy laws before expanding globally. Australia-first also lets us launch faster and iterate based on feedback.
