About This Habit Tracker Printable

This habit tracker printable gives you a clean monthly grid for tracking daily habits on a 5mm dot grid page. Write your habits down the left side, tick or colour each day across the row, and see your consistency build over the month.

It's one of the most popular bullet journal spreads — and now you don't need to draw it by hand every month. Print a fresh one, stick it in your journal or binder, and start tracking. Use colours, stickers, or simple checkmarks — whatever works for you.

What's Included

  • Monthly habit tracking grid (31 days)
  • 5mm dot grid background
  • Available in A4 and A5
  • 300 DPI, print-ready PDF
  • Works with GoodNotes & Notability

Habit Ideas to Get You Started

  • Water intake, exercise, meditation, reading
  • Medication tracking, skincare routine
  • No screen before bed, journaling, stretching
  • Any daily habit you want to build or monitor
