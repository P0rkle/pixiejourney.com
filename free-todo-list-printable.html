About This To-Do List Printable

This to-do list printable combines a structured checkbox task list with a 5mm dot grid note area on the same page. It's designed for bullet journal users who want a clean, reusable task page they can print as often as they need.

Write your tasks in the checkbox section, tick them off as you go, and use the dot grid space for notes, sketches, or overflow items. Print a fresh copy whenever you need one — perfect for weekly resets or project planning.

What's Included
