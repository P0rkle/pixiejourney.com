About This Garden Tracker Printable

This garden patch tracker printable is a versatile adult coloring page designed for bullet journals and planners. Color one garden patch each day to track any habit, mood, or goal across the month. Featuring Lalah the tracking pixie tending her garden, this nature-themed tracker makes daily logging feel like growing something beautiful.

Use it for anything — gratitude, exercise, water intake, mood, energy levels, or medication tracking. The garden theme works beautifully as a metaphor for personal growth, making it especially popular for mental health and wellness tracking.
