About This Mood Tracker Printable

This mood star tracker printable is designed for bullet journals and planners. Color one star each day to track your mood across the month. Featuring Lalah the tracking pixie, this cosy adult coloring tracker makes daily mood logging feel creative and calming.

Each star represents a day of the month. Choose your own colour key — assign colours to moods like happy, calm, anxious, or tired — and fill in one star per day. At the end of the month, you'll see your emotional patterns at a glance.
