Pricing Questions

Is Life Free really free?

Yes! Life Free is completely free forever. No time limit, no credit card required, no hidden fees.

Monthly vs Lifetime?

Monthly ($4.99): Pay as you go, cancel anytime.
Lifetime ($14.99 waitlist): Pay once, use forever. Better value after 3 months.

How does waitlist pricing work?

All waitlist members get Life+ Lifetime for $14.99 instead of $49.99 (save $35). This pricing is locked in—no time limit.

Can I switch from a la carte to a subscription?

Yes! You can upgrade anytime. We'll credit your recent a la carte purchases toward the subscription.

What if I cancel Life+?

You automatically revert to Life Free. You keep all your data but can only actively log the Life Free categories.

Can I get a refund?

Monthly: No refunds for partial months. Lifetime: Generally no refunds, except as required by Australian Consumer Law.
