Privacy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

This is a plain-language summary of how we handle your information. A full legal privacy policy will be published before the app launches. If you have questions, contact us.

What we collect

  • Email address — to send your free tracking PDFs and (if you opt in) early access updates.

That's it for now. When the app launches, we'll collect what's needed to run your account (like login details and your tracking data). We'll update this page before that happens.

How we use it

  • To email your PDF download link
  • To send early access updates (only if you opt in)

We don't use your email for anything else.

What we don't do

  • We don't sell your email address
  • We don't share it with advertisers
  • We don't use it to train AI models
  • We don't send spam

On-device processing

When you use Pixie Journey tools on our website, the processing happens locally in your browser. That means:

  • We do not receive your tool inputs (dates, symptoms, scores, notes)
  • We do not store those inputs on our servers
  • Your entries stay on your device unless you choose to share them

If you download or export a file, it is saved on your device. Be careful when sharing exports, as they may contain personal information.

Cookies and tracking

  • Essential cookies only — we use cookies required for core website functionality and security.
  • Privacy-focused analytics — we use minimal analytics to understand general site usage in aggregate.
  • No advertising pixels — we do not use advertising pixels and we do not sell personal data.

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Third-party services

We may use reputable third-party providers (website hosting, content delivery, email delivery, security). These providers may process limited technical data to deliver the website.

Our website may link to other websites. We are not responsible for the content, security, or privacy practices of third-party sites.

Unsubscribe

You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in any email we send.

Changes to this page

We may update this privacy page from time to time. We will update the date at the top whenever changes are made.

Questions?

Email us at hello@pixiejourney.com.

For security issues: security@pixiejourney.com
