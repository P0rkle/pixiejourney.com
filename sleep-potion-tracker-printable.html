About This Sleep Tracker Printable

This sleep potion tracker printable is designed for bullet journals and planners. Color one potion bottle each night to track your sleep quality across the month. Featuring Lalah the tracking pixie, this whimsical adult coloring tracker turns nightly sleep logging into a calming bedtime ritual.

Assign colours to sleep quality levels — deep sleep, restful, light, restless, or insomnia — and fill in one bottle per day. By the end of the month, you'll have a beautiful visual map of your sleep patterns.
