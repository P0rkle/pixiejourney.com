What Pixie Journey is

Pixie Journey is a Life in Pixels tracking system — printable PDFs now, and a digital app coming Q3 2026. It helps you track mood, sleep, and daily life in a gentle, visual way.

What it isn't

  • It's not medical advice or a medical device
  • It's not a substitute for professional healthcare
  • It's not contraception — don't use fertility tracking to prevent pregnancy
  • It's not a crisis service — if you're in danger, call 000 (Australia) or your local emergency number

Free tracking PDFs

  • The printable PDFs are free for personal use
  • You can use them in classrooms and clinics
  • You can't resell or redistribute them

Your data

  • Right now, we only collect your email (if you sign up for PDFs or the waitlist)
  • We don't sell your data
  • You can unsubscribe anytime
  • When the app launches, your tracking data will be yours — stored locally on your device by default

See our Privacy page for more detail.

App pricing (when it launches)

  • Life Free: $0 — core tracking, free forever
  • Life+: All 20+ categories, no ads, data export. Pricing details before launch.
  • Clinical: Coming 2027. For chronic condition management.

Waitlist members will get special pricing when the app launches.

Australian law

Pixie Journey is based in Australia. Nothing in these terms overrides your rights under Australian Consumer Law.
